    Our Story

    Y.N.K Logos Consulting & Services Ltd was established with a clear vision - to bring international quality lubrication and maintenance solutions to the Cypriot market, combining professionalism, availability, and competitive prices.
    The company specializes in importing and marketing advanced oils and maintenance fluids for light vehicles, heavy vehicles, and industry, serving as the representative and distributor of the German oil brand Maximum. The company's products are manufactured to the highest standards and meet OEM specifications, European standards, and strict international regulations.
    We work directly with the manufacturer in Germany, which enables us to ensure consistent quality, high availability, and solutions tailored to the needs of the local market in Cyprus. Our extensive product range serves garages, vehicle fleets, transportation companies, and industrial customers - all under one roof.
    Our commitment is to long-term partnerships with our customers, based on reliability, professional knowledge, and personal service, alongside quality products that provide performance, protection, and long-term savings.

    Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability and excellence in the field of German automotive oils. We are proud to have become the preferred supplier for garages, transport companies, fleet owners and private vehicle owners throughout Cyprus. Our success stems from our unwavering commitment to quality, excellent service and customer satisfaction.
