    Our Services and Products

    Engine Oils for Private Vehicles

    We supply a wide range of synthetic and semi-synthetic engine oils for private cars and SUVs. All our products meet the strictest ACEA and API standards and are approved by leading car manufacturers worldwide. Our oils ensure maximum engine protection and reduce wear.

    Our products include oils in various viscosity grades (0W-20, 5W-30, 5W-40, 10W-40 and more) suitable for all engine types - gasoline, diesel, turbo and hybrid. We also offer special oils for sports cars and high-performance vehicles.

    Heavy and Commercial Vehicle Oils

    Our special expertise is in supplying oils for light and heavy vehicles Cyprus, including trucks, buses, tractors and heavy equipment. We offer engine oils with high resistance to heavy loads, extreme temperatures and harsh working conditions.

    Our heavy vehicle oils meet Euro VI standards and are suitable for modern diesel engines with advanced emission control systems. We also supply oils for agricultural equipment, forklifts and construction machinery, with precise adaptation to manufacturer requirements and usage conditions.

    Transmission and Gearbox Oils

    Complete range of oils for automatic transmissions (ATF), manual (MTF) and differential. Our oils ensure smooth power transmission, reduce noise and extend transmission life. We offer products adapted to all transmission types - conventional, CVT, DSG and advanced automatic transmissions.

    Additionally, we supply special oils for heavy and commercial vehicle transmissions, including oils with high resistance to pressure and high temperatures. All our products meet GL-4, GL-5 standards and specific manufacturer requirements.

    Brake and Hydraulic Fluids

    Vehicle safety is our top priority, which is why we offer only the highest quality brake fluids. Our products meet DOT 3 and DOT 4 standards and are suitable for all brake system types - conventional, ABS, ESP and advanced electronic braking systems.

    We also supply hydraulic oils for power steering systems, hydraulic suspensions and hydraulic systems in heavy machinery. Our fluids ensure optimal performance across the entire temperature range, high resistance to oxidation and wear, and full compatibility with all system components.
